Instructions

Paul Tré, the chicken, has been chosen for the butchery at a local farm in Idaho. The crusty old farmer, James, plucked out all of his feathers, leaving him vulnerable and naked. Paul Tré managed to escape the butchery but needs your help navigating the farm to look for his missing feathers. Once Paul Tré finds all his feathers, he can go back to being a normal chicken and have the confidence to advance to the other levels and eventually escape. Watch out for the rotating guard that will take you back if he sees you! Move Paul Tré with the right and left arrow keys to avoid obstacles such as fences and hazards that will move you back to the start.
Help boost Paul Trés confidence!
